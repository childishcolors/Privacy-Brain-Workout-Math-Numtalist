Privacy Policy
Brain Workout Math: Numtalist

1. Introduction
Welcome to Brain Workout Math ("the App", "we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

By using Brain Workout Math: Numtalist, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect
2.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device
Brain Workout Math stores your progress data locally on your device only. This includes:

XP points, level, and streak counts
Daily goals and daily practice counts
Skill level ratings per category (Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, Division, Mixed)
Lesson progress and completion status
Preferred language setting
Pro subscription status (synced from RevenueCat)
This data is stored in your device's local storage and is never transmitted to our servers.

2.2 Information Collected by Third-Party Services
We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. When you make a purchase or restore a purchase, RevenueCat may collect:

A unique user/device identifier (generated by RevenueCat)
Purchase transaction data (handled entirely by Apple)
Subscription status and entitlements
For more information: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

3. How We Use Your Information
We use locally stored data solely to display your progress, unlock lessons, remember your settings, and determine your Pro status. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or analytics.

4. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions
Billing: Charged to your Apple ID upon confirmation.
Auto-renewal: Renews unless cancelled at least 24 hours before period end.
Cancellation: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
Restore: Use "Restore Purchases" in the Paywall or Settings screen.
5. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We do not sell or trade your personal information. We share data only with RevenueCat (subscription management) and Apple (payment processing), and when required by law.

6. Data Retention and Deletion
All data is on-device only. Delete it anytime via Settings → Data → Reset all progress, or by uninstalling the App.

7. Children's Privacy
The App is suitable for all ages. We do not collect names, emails, or any personally identifiable information, and we do not display behavioral ads or allow user-to-user communication.

8. Security
Progress data is secured by your device's own protections (Face ID, passcode, etc.). Subscription verification uses industry-standard encryption via RevenueCat and Apple.

9. Your Rights
You may access, correct, or delete your data at any time. Since no personal data is stored on our servers, most requests are fulfilled directly within the App.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We will update the "Last updated" date when changes are made. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
